MusicOn

Mood recognition music player mobile app.

MusicOn is a mood recognition music player, developed in aim of improving road safety by improving the mood of the drivers to a more calm and stable state.

The MusicOn app uses machine learning techniques to determine the mood of the user. The app access the front camera of the phone to take a photo of the user and directs it to a ML model to identify the current mood. Then the songs playlist will be arranged depending on the current mood to get the user to a more calm state.

Marine Fish Price Prediction System

ML based price prediction system

The system forecasts the monthly average whole sale marine fish prices in colombo. Ths system is developed to predict the average monthly price of 5 selected marine fish types for the upcoming month.

Developed system uses sliding window method to frame the time series forecasting in to real valued supervised learning problem. The needed data to train the model is obtaind from the statistic department of the ministry of fisheries and aquatic resource development.
